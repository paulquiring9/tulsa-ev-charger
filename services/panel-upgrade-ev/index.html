Electrical Panel Upgrades for EV Chargers in Tulsa

Last verified: May 2026

Many Tulsa homes — especially those built before the 1990s — have 100-amp electrical panels that can't safely support a Level 2 EV charger without an upgrade. An electrician can assess your panel in about 15 minutes.

Do You Need a Panel Upgrade?

You likely need an upgrade if your home has a 100-amp service panel, your panel is full with no available breaker slots, or your panel is older than 30 years. You probably don't need one if you have a 200-amp panel with available space. How to check your panel →

Cost of a Panel Upgrade in Tulsa

A standard 200-amp panel upgrade in Tulsa runs $2,000–$3,500 including labor, materials, permits, and inspection. Combined with the EV charger installation, expect $2,500–$4,500 total for the full project.
