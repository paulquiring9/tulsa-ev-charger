Level 2 EV Charger Installation in Tulsa, Oklahoma

Last verified: May 2026

A Level 2 charger installation is the right choice for virtually every Tulsa homeowner. It uses a 240V dedicated circuit — the same type of outlet as a dryer or range — and can fully charge most electric vehicles in 4–8 hours overnight.

A licensed Tulsa electrician runs a dedicated circuit from your electrical panel to your garage or parking area, mounts the charger, and schedules the required City of Tulsa inspection. The whole job typically takes 2–4 hours.

What's Included

  • Dedicated 240V circuit installation
  • Charger mounting and wiring (hardwired or outlet)
  • Permit pulled from City of Tulsa
  • Inspection coordination
  • Itemized receipt for tax credit filing

Typical Cost in Tulsa

Most straightforward Level 2 installs in Tulsa run $900–$1,400 total including the charger unit. Homes needing a panel upgrade first run $2,500–$4,000+. See full cost breakdown →

Compatible With All EV Brands

Tesla, Ford, Chevy, Kia, Hyundai, Rivian, BMW, Mercedes — a Level 2 installation works with any electric vehicle. We also install Tesla Wall Connectors specifically for Tesla owners who want the native charging experience.
