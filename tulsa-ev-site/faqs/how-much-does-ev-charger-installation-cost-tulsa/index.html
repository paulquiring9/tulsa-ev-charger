How Much Does EV Charger Installation Cost in Tulsa?

Last verified: May 2026

A standard Level 2 EV charger installation in Tulsa costs $900–$1,400 total for a home with a ready panel. That includes $500–$900 in electrician labor, $300–$430 for the charger unit, and $30–$80 for the permit. Homes needing a panel upgrade first run $2,500–$4,000+.

ScenarioEstimated Cost
Simple install, 200A panel ready$900–$1,200
Longer conduit run or outdoor mount$1,200–$1,800
Panel upgrade + charger install$2,500–$4,500
Commercial / multi-unit installCustom quote

What's Included in the Price

  • Electrician labor (typically 2–4 hours)
  • Level 2 charger unit (if supplied by electrician)
  • Dedicated 240V circuit materials
  • City of Tulsa electrical permit
  • Inspection coordination
