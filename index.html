Federal Tax Credit

You could get up to $1,000 back — here's how

Most Tulsa homeowners have heard there's a federal tax credit for EV charger installation. Very few understand how it actually works, whether their address qualifies, or how to claim it.

The credit is 30% of your total installation cost, up to $1,000. It's a tax credit — not a rebate — so you claim it on IRS Form 8911 when you file your federal return. Your electrician doesn't file it for you. And not every Tulsa address qualifies.
