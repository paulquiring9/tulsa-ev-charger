EV Charger Installation in Tulsa, Oklahoma

Last verified: May 2026

Tulsa is Oklahoma's second-largest city and one of the fastest-growing EV markets in the state. PSO (Public Service Company of Oklahoma) serves the Tulsa metro area — PSO customers are eligible for a $200 rebate on qualifying Level 2 smart chargers.

Tulsa follows the statewide adoption of the 2023 National Electrical Code (NEC), including Article 625 for EV charging equipment. All installations require a permit from the City of Tulsa — your licensed electrician handles this.
